Manufacturing Process of the Laptop:

1. **Component Sourcing**: High-quality components such as processors, memory modules, and displays are sourced from leading suppliers worldwide.  
2. **Assembly**: The Laptop undergoes precise assembly using robotic automation, ensuring consistent quality.  
3. **Quality Testing**: Each unit is subjected to rigorous testing, including stress tests, drop tests, and thermal performance checks.  
4. **Environmental Compliance**: The manufacturing process adheres to strict environmental standards, including reduced emissions and the use of recyclable materials.  
5. **Final Inspection**: A manual inspection ensures that each Laptop meets the company's high standards before packaging and distribution.  
6. **Packaging and Shipping**: Packaged with eco-friendly materials and optimized for safe and efficient global shipping.
